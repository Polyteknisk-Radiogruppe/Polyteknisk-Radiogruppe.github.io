PRG launch his new website

  • 20 November, 2021

We are launching a new website with updated information about our radiogrouppe.

PRG is a club for all DTU students interested in HAM radio communication and electronics. The club is located at DTU building 101 E, 1 floor, room 116C. We have a club day every Wednesday at 17:30, where you can meet a board member who can help you sign into the club. The price for membership pr semester is 60kr.- for students and 100kr.- for non-students.

If you want to contact us, please write to this email: dtu.prg@gmail.com
