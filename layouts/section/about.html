About

How to find us

PRG is a club for all DTU students interested in electronics, radio communication, programming, machine learning and much more! The club is located at DTU building 101 E, 1 floor, room 116C. We have a club day every Wednesday at 17:30, where you can meet a board member who can help you sign into the club

We can be contacted via e-mail at prg@prg.dtu.dk.
