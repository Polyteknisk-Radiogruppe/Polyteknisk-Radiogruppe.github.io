ABOUT US: PRG is at its core an opportunity to learn by doing – we support our members in developing their skills within cyber physical engineering (embedded systems, radio technology, robotics, drones etc.), not just by making them better engineers but also by giving them the opportunity to grow as leaders, professionals and friends.

 PHYSICAL AMENITIES:
 * Workshop with everything you need to develop your cool projects. Including but not limited to; 3D printers, oscilloscopes, power supplies, signal generators, shit ton of components, multi meters and so much more! Thanks to our sponsors we can also offer Nordic Semiconductor development kits for your projects.
 * LoRa and hexbeam antennae on the roof for developing IoT or radio technology projects. You may also install your own antenna on the roof.
 * Server room for hosting your own website, running an IoT server, machine learning or whatever you may need. If you want to learn how to operate a server, you’re also more than welcome to contact our sys-admin.
 
Opportunities:
 * Hackathons
 * Hosting 1 day courses at Skylab
 * Larger projects with companies and other organizations where you can meet new people, both other students but also working professionals, and work on cool stuff  together (W.I.P).
 * Trips to companies  (W.I.P)
